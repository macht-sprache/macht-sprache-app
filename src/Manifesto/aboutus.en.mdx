## About us

Many voices and views have contributed to the making of this tool. The knowledge and content that have flowed into its development were collaboratively produced via a platform built for this purpose, as well as by drawing insights from various expert voices who work with language(s) in various ways including Mirjam Nuenning, Michaela Dudley, Lann Hornscheidt, Şeyda Kurt, Annabelle Assaf, Amy Zayed, Khairani Barokka, x, z, … and many others.

The project was initiated and the final tool’s recommendations curated by Lucy Gasser and Anna von Rath of poco.lit., and Timur Celikel and Kolja Lange…
