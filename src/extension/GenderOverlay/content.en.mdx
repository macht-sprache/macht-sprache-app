These terms describing people are highlighted so that you can consciously consider how to gender them in translation.
In English, such terms are grammatically gender-neutral. In German, they are likely to be assigned a grammatical gender.

There are several options for gender-inclusive expression in German. Variants of gender-inclusive noun formulations include
the internal I, the underscore, the asterisk and the colon.
